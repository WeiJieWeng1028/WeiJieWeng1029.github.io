𝗔𝗕𝗢𝗨𝗧｜關於我

我是煒傑｜Wei Jie，一位來自港都高雄的自由影像創作者。 我喜歡用鏡頭記錄生活裡微小卻真實的時刻，從劇情片、紀錄片到廣告，在每一段創作歷程中，我總是試圖捕捉那些容易被忽略的情感與氛圍。 過去曾參與各類型的影像製作，擔任攝影、攝影助理等職務，也持續拍攝屬於自己的作品。 期待透過影像讓觀眾看見另一種觀看世界的方式，並與更多創作者產生連結。。

Hi, I'm Wei Jie, a freelance visual creator from the port city of Kaohsiung. I enjoy using the camera to capture the small yet genuine moments in life. From narrative films and documentaries to commercial shoots, I always strive to portray the often-overlooked emotions and atmospheres throughout every creative journey. In the past, I’ve taken part in various productions as a cinematographer and camera assistant, while also continuing to develop my own projects. Through images, I hope to offer audiences a different way of seeing the world—and to connect with more creators along the way.
